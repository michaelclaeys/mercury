Program Rules
Rules & Requirements
Mercury Funding operates like a managed capital program. These rules protect both you and us. Read them carefully before applying.
Eligibility
✓
Active Pro subscription — You must be on the Pro plan ($99/mo) at the time of application and throughout the funded period.
✓
Verified track record — All performance data must come from live trading on Mercury. Backtests alone do not qualify. Paper trading does not qualify.
✓
Account in good standing — No prior violations of Mercury's terms of service. No history of market manipulation or wash trading.
✓
Single strategy per application — Each funding application is tied to one strategy. You may apply with multiple strategies separately.
Capital & Profit Split
●
80/20 profit split — You keep 80% of net profits generated with funded capital. Mercury retains 20%. Payouts are calculated monthly.
●
No upfront fees — There is no evaluation fee or challenge fee. Your Pro subscription is the only cost. Mercury Funding is a reward, not a product.
●
Capital stays on Mercury — Funded capital is deposited into your Mercury trading wallet and can only be used for bot execution on the platform. It cannot be withdrawn.
●
Profits are yours — Your 80% profit share is withdrawable to your personal wallet at any time after each monthly settlement.
Risk Limits
✕
Maximum daily drawdown: 5% — If your funded strategy loses more than 5% of allocated capital in a single day, trading is automatically paused for 24 hours.
✕
Maximum total drawdown: 10% — If cumulative losses reach 10% of funded capital, the allocation is revoked. You may reapply after 30 days with a new track record.
✕
No martingale or grid strategies — Strategies that double down on losing positions or rely on unlimited capital are not eligible.
✕
No single-market concentration > 40% — You cannot allocate more than 40% of funded capital to any single prediction market contract.
✕
No manual override of funded bots — Funded strategies must run autonomously. Manual trades on funded capital are not permitted.
Monitoring & Compliance
●
Real-time monitoring — Mercury monitors all funded strategies 24/7. Automated risk controls enforce daily and total drawdown limits.
●
Monthly performance reviews — Funded accounts are reviewed monthly. Consistently profitable strategies may be offered higher allocations.
●
Strategy transparency — Mercury can view your strategy's node graph and execution logs. We will never share, copy, or redistribute your strategy.
●
Revocation — Mercury reserves the right to revoke funding at any time if rules are violated, performance degrades significantly, or your Pro subscription lapses.
Scaling
✓
Performance-based increases — Funded traders who maintain profitability for 60+ consecutive days are eligible for capital increases up to the next tier.
✓
Multi-strategy funding — Elite-tier traders may apply for funding on additional strategies. Maximum total allocation across all strategies: $10,000.
✓
No cap on earnings — There is no maximum profit limit. The more your strategy earns, the more you take home.
