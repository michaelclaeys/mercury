About Mercury

Built by forecasters,
for forecasters.

We built Mercury because we needed it. Every feature exists because we hit a wall trading prediction markets with inadequate tools. No feature bloat. No guesswork.

The Problem

Prediction markets are the most efficient pricing mechanism for real-world events ever created. But the tooling around them is years behind traditional finance. No real-time greeks. No cross-platform views. No automation. Traders are making decisions with spreadsheets and browser tabs.

Our Approach

Mercury brings institutional-grade analytics, automation, and infrastructure to prediction markets. Real-time probability tracking, cross-platform arbitrage detection, AI-powered bot building, and production-grade execution — all in a single terminal interface designed for speed.

Element 80

Mercury — element 80 on the periodic table. The only metal that's liquid at room temperature. Fast, fluid, and adaptive. That's what prediction market tooling should be. Liquid intelligence.

Principles

Speed Over Everything
